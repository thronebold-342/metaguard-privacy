# MetaGuard Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

## Overview
MetaGuard is a privacy protection app that detects nearby Meta Ray-Ban glasses using Bluetooth scanning. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data We Collect
MetaGuard does NOT collect, store, or share any personal data. All scanning happens entirely on your device.

Specifically:
- We do not collect your location
- We do not collect Bluetooth scan results
- We do not store any device information
- We do not share any data with third parties
- We do not have any servers or databases

## Bluetooth Usage
MetaGuard uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) scanning and BLE fingerprinting to detect the unique broadcast patterns that Meta Ray-Ban glasses emit. This allows detection even when the glasses are already paired to another device. All scanning happens entirely on your device and no data ever leaves it.

## Notifications
MetaGuard sends local notifications when a Meta device is detected nearby. These notifications are generated on your device and are not sent to any external server.

## Background Activity
When Background Monitoring is enabled, MetaGuard runs in the background to scan for nearby devices. This activity stays entirely on your device.

## Children's Privacy
MetaGuard does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page.
